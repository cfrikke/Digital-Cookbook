3 cups of Flour

1 teaspoons of Baking Soda

1 cup White Sugar

1 Cup

0.5 Teaspoons Salt

2 Cups Chocolate Chips

2 Teaspoons Vanilla Extract

2 Teaspoons Hot Water

1 Cup Softened Butter

2 Eggs


Step 1

Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened.

Step 2

Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).

Step 3

Beat butter, white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer in a large bowl until smooth.

Step 4

Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.

Step 5

Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt.

Step 6

Stir in flour and chocolate chips.

Step 7

Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.

Step 8

Bake in the oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.

Step 9

Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.

Step 10

Store in an airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!

TLDR

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour and chocolate chips.
  4. drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet.
  5. Bake untilthe edges are golden brown.
